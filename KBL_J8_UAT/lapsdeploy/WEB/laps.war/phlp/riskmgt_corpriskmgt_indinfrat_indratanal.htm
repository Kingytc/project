Industries Information and Rating (CoRc)

Industry Rating Analysis Tab
Various parameters and sub-parameters for assessing the industry performance are displayed in this screen so that the Risk management team can evaluate the industry on the basis of these parameters. The scores corresponding to the Rating is automatically populated. These scores are later populated in the “Rating model” for the final evaluation of the applicant. There may be multiple sections under the Industry module, for instance, the Industry & Economy & The Industry section.

Industry - Various industries and their respective CMA’s are attached to the relevant “Rating model”. For instance, Potential borrowers belonging to “Diamond & Jewelry” industry, which is part of the “CMA Risk management”, will be rated using the “Comprehensive Rating model”
Industry- Click the “?” next to the text box
CMA - Click the “?” next to the text box
A small popup window displays a list of all CMA’ s
Select the CMA to which above mentioned Industry belongs
Rating model - Various Rating models created previously are listed in this dropdown box for the user to attach the Industry in consideration
Click the “?” next to the text rating model text box
Select the rating model to which the Industry and CMA are attached
Date - The date when the record was created is recorded here in this date field
Click “GO”
The entire set of parameters that have been defined under “Industry & economy” section (by the risk management department) is displayed
Click “Edit”
Rating - There is a “rating” dropdown box corresponding to each parameter.
Click the dropdown box
Select the rating for each parameter
The score gets automatically populated
Enter remarks, if any, in the empty text box provided
Click “Save” to save the changes made
Click “cancel” to cancel the operation
Click the next tab or click “Close” to close the screen and go back to the “commercial rating screen


To edit existing details
Select the Industry, CMA and Rating model
Click Edit
Make changes in “Rating” and Remarks
Click “Save” to save the changes made
Click “cancel” to cancel the operation
Click the next tab or click “Close” to close the screen and go back to the “commercial rating screen
