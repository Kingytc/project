Mobility of Individual - Location Tab
Business rule ID - The loan product that was selected in “Step 1a” remains automatically selected. You can change it if required by just clicking the dropdown menu.
Now enter points for 3 different situations of mobility of the applicant or in other words points are awarded based on how long he has stayed in a particular location. The parameters are not editable but the points are soft in nature. In our example, for the housing loan
Location Points Description
Has not changed location in past 3 years 4
Changed location once in past 3 years 2
Changed location more than once in past 3 years 0

Enter description if necessary
