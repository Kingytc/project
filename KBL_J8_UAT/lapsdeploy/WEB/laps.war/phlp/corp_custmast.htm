Creating a new customer profile
This is the master screen where the basic demographic details of the corporate applicant are obtained. The key field in this field is the “Industry” to which he belongs. Because the CMA format, Financials and the Rating model have all been created based on various Industries only. Other information collected includes; Name of the company, Industry BSR code, Activity, Type of ownership, the parent Group, Corporate office address, Factory address, registered office address etc. Location of the applicant is a key field to enable search functions in the system.
