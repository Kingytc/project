Retail Products

    To creating a retail loan product
    You will see 3 options under “Products”, namely:
    Category master
    Terms and conditions
    Products

The function of this screen is creating loan categories. Category in Retail loans is the umbrella of products, which can hold more than one sub-classification under it. For example, “Housing Loan” is a category while; Home improvement, Loan for constructing a new house, loan for buying an existing house would serve as its sub- categories

Click “Category master”
