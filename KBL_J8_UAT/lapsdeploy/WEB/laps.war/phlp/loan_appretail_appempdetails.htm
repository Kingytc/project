Employment details

    Current employment details
    Employer Name - Enter the organization where the applicant' is currently employed. This is an alphanumeric field.
    Address - The employer’s full address is required to be filled in here. This is again a alphanumeric field
    Designation - This is a mandatory field. Click the dropdown box
    Choose from the choices displayed; Executive, managerial, junior/ clerical
    Department - Enter the department in which the applicant serves in his current organization. This is an optional alphanumeric field
    Years with employer - Enter the number of years the applicant has been with the current employer in this numeric field
    Contact person - Enter a reference in the applicant's current organization, For e.g., his/ her immediate supervisor. This is a mandatory field
    Employer’s e-mail - Enter the email ID of the “contact person”. This is an optional alphanumeric field
    Previous employer and City - Name and city of the applicant’s previous employer, if any is to be entered in this optional alphanumeric field.
    Retirement Age - Enter the applicant's retirement age in the present organization. This is a mandatory numeric field

    Click "Apply" to save the details entered or Click "Cancel" to cancel the operation
