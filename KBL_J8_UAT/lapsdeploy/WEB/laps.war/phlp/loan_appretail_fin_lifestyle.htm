Lifestyle Tab

Click " Lifestyle" tab.
Select “Yes” or “No” for the items displayed in the screen
This tab captures the “lifestyle” of the applicant.
The items included in this tab are Car, Two-wheeler, Refrigerator, Telephone, Computer,
Cell phone, Washing Machine.
Click on the dropdown boxes
There is a list box next to each of these items, Choose “yes” or “No” based on what the
applicant owns.
