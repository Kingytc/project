Investments Tab

Click "Investments" sub-tab
Enter data in the columns provided
Description - Enter the name of the financial institution or bank or any other relevant organization where the investment has been made and also enter the type of investment here. The investment could be in the form of deposits or purchased shares/ stocks/ debentures. This is an alphanumeric field
Market/ present value - The market/ present value of each such unit of share/ stock/ debenture/ investment made is recorded here
No of Units - The total number of shares/ stocks/ debentures or units is recorded here. This is a numeric field. In the event, this product is skipped, by default it will take the value “” in this field
Total value - This is a calculated field, where the value arriving out of multiplying the “purchase/ face value” and “no. units” automatically appears
Applicant type - This investment could belong to either the applicant/ co-applicant or the guarantor. This dropdown box lets you choose whom the account belongs to. If the co-applicant or guarantor are not present for this application then they do not figure on the list
Collateral - Check the radio button next to the bank account, which you want to consider as collateral. Any of these bank accounts can be offered as collateral irrespective of whether the account belongs to the applicant/ co-applicant/ guarantor.

Once all required Investment details are entered, click Apply then Click the next tab.
