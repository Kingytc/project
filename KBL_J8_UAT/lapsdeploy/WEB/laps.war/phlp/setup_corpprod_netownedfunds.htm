Net owned funds

    Bank net owned funds
    Click “Edit” to modify existing record
    Mention bank’s “net owned funds” in Rupees
    Single borrower exposure limit cannot exceed a particular percentage of the bank’s net owned funds. You can mention that percentage in this screen
    In the same way “Group exposure Limit” cannot exceed a particular percentage of the bank’s net owned funds. You can mention that percentage in this screen
    Modified date - Since the bank has the choice to modify “Net owned funds”, this field has been provided. The date on which figures were changed has to be noted

    Click “Apply” to save changes
    Click “Cancel” if you do not wish to save the changes carried out
    Click “Close to get back to the “Corporate products” screen
