Credit Rating Raster

Borrower Ratio Score Range Tab

The ratios can be for “Historical” or “Projected” years. Historical is the last five years leaving out the current year. While projections is for the next 5 years excluding the current year. If “Historical” is chosen, the average of the last five years is taken and compared with that of last year. And in the same way if “Projections” is chosen then the average of the next 5 years is compared with the next years ratio
The value so arrived at is compared to the range given in this screen and the corresponding “point” is taken into the Rating model as the score

Create a new Borrower ratio score range
CMA - Click the “?” next to the CMA text box
A small popup window displays the entire list of CMA’s
Select the CMA for which you wish to create the ratio score range
Ratio - Click the”?” next to the Ratio text box
Select the ratio
Now click ”Edit”
Range from - range to - Enter the range within which the borrower ratio calculated can fall
Point -Enter the point for each range. In other words, when a borrower financial ratio is calculated, for example, the borrowers “Historical TDE” ratio might fall into the range “1.01-1.25”. There will be a point/ score that the borrower will earn. Enter those points in this field.
Click “Save” to save the changes made
Click “cancel” to cancel the operation
Click the next tab or click “Close” to close the screen and go back to the “commercial rating screen”

Edit an existing “Score range”
Select the CMA
Select the Ratio
Click “List”
The ranges are displayed in the table where the data was originally entered
Click “Edit”
Make necessary changes
Click “Save” to save the changes made
Click “cancel” to cancel the operation
Click the next tab or click “Close” to close the screen and go back to the “commercial rating screen”
