Appraisal

Two sub-tabs are displayed, namely, “Terms & conditions" and "Appraisal"

Terms & conditions
The control is in "Terms & conditions" by default
Necessary terms and conditions have been already created for each loan, this list is displayed in this screen. Along with this new terms can also be appended depending on the applicant. The loan officer when required can also delete any such term added.

Add Terms and a condition - Use this to add new terms & conditions that is applicant specific.
Click "Add" button in the bottom of the page. Now you can enter the new terms & conditions in the box provided
Click the Apply button to save the entry made
To delete any of the terms:
Click on the round radio button next to the term you would like to delete
Click the "Delete" button in the bottom of the page
If you are trying to delete an already existing term (and not a newly appended one) then a popup window appears
Enter the reason for deleting the term
Click "ok" to save the reason
Click "Close" if the reason entered need not be saved, In which case the deletion will not happen
Deleted Terms - Click here to view the list of deleted terms along with the reason for such an action.
