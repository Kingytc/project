Other asset

A maximum of 10 assets can be entered in this screen. Enter details in the various columns provided:
Details of Asset - Enter the description of the proposed asset in this alphanumeric field
Name of supplier/ dealer - Enter the name of the supplier or dealer (whichever is applicable depending on the nature of the asset
Purchase price/ amount (Rs) - Enter the purchase price in this numeric field
sMarket value - Enter the market value of the asset in this numeric field
