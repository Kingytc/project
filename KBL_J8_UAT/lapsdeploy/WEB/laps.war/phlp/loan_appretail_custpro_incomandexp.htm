Income & expenses
Gross income - Enter the applicant's total undetected income earned from his primary source of occupation (the one that is mentioned in the demographics tab)
Other Income - Enter income, if any from rent, agriculture or any other source. This is an optional numeric field. For Housing Loans, in case of Professionals, self employed and business persons, the figures to be filled in, on this page should be the average of last three years.
Other monthly expenditure - Enter details on vehicle loan repayments, etc
Total income - This is a calculated field:

Deductions. There is a mouse over to display the parameters that go into the
Calculation of deduction.
(-) Other deductions (rent + total loan repayment)
(=) Net income
(+) Other income (agriculture + rent + Others)
(=) Total income
Description - Any description on expenses can be entered. This is an optional alphanumeric field
