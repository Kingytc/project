Facility Master
Click "Facility master"

    Head of facility - This is the field where the major facility heads are chosen to create various other facilities under them. There are 4 baskets of financial services which are termed as "FACILITY HEADS" namely, Term Finance Funded, Term Finance non-funded, Working capital funded, Working capital contingent.

    Facility - Each Facility head created can accommodate multiple facilities under it. Such facilities can be created in this alphanumeric field. For instance, Post shipment term loan, Foreign currency term loan under "TERM FINANCE FUNDED"

    Code - A unique identification code created by the Bank for each of the facility heads and facilities under them. "WC" and "TF" should be used for Working Capital and Term Finance compulsorily
