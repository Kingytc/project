Financial
Click "Financial" tab
6 tabs are displayed, namely,
Bank accounts
Investments
Insurance policies
Personal properties
Liabilities
Lifestyle.

Click Edit button to enter / modify data in one or more of the above sub tabs. Enter financial details in order and no blank lines should be left between entries.

Control rests in "Bank accounts" by default. But you can enter information in any of the other tabs as well. Just Click "Cancel" (if you haven't entered any data in bank accounts) and then Click the "tab" you want to go to. Basically choose those tabs where the applicant has relevant information to enter. The assets mentioned could be offered as collateral irrespective of whether it belongs to applicant, co-applicant or guarantor.

Bank Accounts Tab

Control will be in Bank accounts” sub-tab by default
This sub-tab collects the banking details of the applicant. This includes the complete profile of the bank and the branch in which he/ she maintains an account with.
Enter data in the columns provided
Bank name/ branch name/ telephone number - Enter the name of the bank with which the applicant has been maintaining accounts. This can be more than one bank. Details collected include, bank name, branch name, telephone number. This is an alphanumeric field.
Applicant type - This dropdown box lets you choose who the account belongs to, i.e., the applicant, co-applicant or the guarantor
No of years Account held - Enter the number of years the applicant/ co-applicant has been maintaining an account with the aforesaid bank
Type of account - Choose from a dropdown list whether it is a savings, current account, cumulative, recurring deposit or a fixed deposit
Account Balance - Enter the Current balance in the bank account. This is a numeric field
Collateral - Check the radio button next to the bank account, which you want to consider as collateral. Any of these bank accounts can be offered as collateral irrespective of whether the account belongs to the applicant/ co-applicant/ guarantor.

Once all required bank account details are entered, click Apply then Click the next tab.
