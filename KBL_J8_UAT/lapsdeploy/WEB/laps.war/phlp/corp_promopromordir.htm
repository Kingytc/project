Promoter

Promoter/Director Tab
You can enter data on any number of promoters who belong to the corporate applicant
Adding a promoter
Select principal - Click the drop down box
Select “New”
Type - Click the dropdown box
Select the type of role he/ she has in the company, the options are:
Promoters
Guarantors
Directors
Shareholders
First, Middle & last Name - Enter the full name of the promoter. For example, Sanghi Ram Singh
Address 1 - Enter the complete address of the promoter including city, state, Pincode. Address 2 has been given incase the space provided in “Address 1” is not enough.
Phone, Fax, email - Enter the number of every available mode of communication with the promoter, namely, phone, Fax & email
Share (%) - Enter the percentage of share he/ she carries in the organization (loan applicant). For example, 25% share in Sanghavi Enterprises
Shares held - Enter the total number of shares, he/ she holds in the organization. For example, 10000 shares in Sanghavi Enterprises
Netwoth - Enter the personal networth of the promoter. For example, Rs 20 Crores
Click “Apply” to save the data entered
Click “cancel” if you want to cancel the operation. Remember that you will lose any data entered.
Click “close” to go back to the search screen

Editing existing promoter details
Click the “Select principal” dropdown box
The names of all promoters entered will be displayed in the dropdown box
Select the name of the promoter whose details you wish to edit
Click “Edit”
Make necessary changes
Click “Apply” to save the data entered
Click “cancel” if you want to cancel the operation. Remember that you will lose any changes made in the record
Click “close” to go back to the search screen
