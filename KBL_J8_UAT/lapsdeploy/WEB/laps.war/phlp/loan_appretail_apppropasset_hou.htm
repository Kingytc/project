Proposed Asset

This section is for entering elaborate details on the asset for which the loan is being requested. Since the proposed asset could be anything, there are 3 sub-tabs, namely House, Vehicle and other asset.
Click the sub-tab, which is depending on the requirement.

House Tab
Address - The complete mailing address of the asset that has been offered as primary security including the city, State, Pincode
Survey No - The survey number for the property allotted by the local governing body, this is a unique identification number
Builder's name - Enter the name of the construction company/ or builder who is going to build the asset or who has built the property if it's an existing property
Property type - Click the drop down box
Choose between the two options, namely, Existing, New
Stage of Property - Click the drop down box
Choose between the options, namely, Planning stage, Foundation stage, intermediate stage, Final stage
Age of property - Enter the age of the building if it is an existing property
Total area - Enter the total spread of the property including the built-up area and free spaces in and around the built-up area that is part of the property
Built up area - Enter the area of the place utilized for living or in other words carpet area
Quality of construction - Enter information about the quality of construction of the proposed asset.
Click the drop down box
Choose between the displayed options, namely, Very Good, Good, Fair, poor
Location value - Real estate value depends on the location, for instance a Duplex apartment in Andheri might cost much more than a similar duplex apartment in Malabar Hill.
Click the drop down box
Choose between the displayed options, namely, Very Good, Good, Fair, poor
Presence of amenities - The presence of amenities might change from property to property; select the amenities available for the proposed asset.
Click the drop down box
Choose between the displayed options, namely, Very Good, Good, Fair, poor
Marketability - The marketability of a property might depend on many issue, location, presence of amenities, market conditions, Quality of construction etc. Decide on the ease with which the property can be marketed and select from the displayed options in the drop down box
Valuation date - Enter the date on which the value of the property was assessed
Valuation done by - Enter the name of the authorized professional who has carried out the valuation
Last Property tax paid - Enter the date in which the property tax was paid for the asset
Last water tax paid - The date in which the tax was last paid for the asset
