Personal Details
A screen appears with the sub-tabs namely, Age, educational qualification, marital status, mobility of individual, No. of. Dependents. Each of the sub-tab carries scores that will vary according to the loan product. The aggregate of all these scores will be taken as the final score on the applicant ’personal details”

Age
By default the control will be in this sub-tab
Business rule ID - Click the dropdown box
Select the loan product
Enter various age ranges and the points in the boxes provided. The age range and points should be picked up from the official directive (rating model) approved by the board.
Lets take an example, if an applicant has asked for a housing loan then based on his age, he could be earning the following points:
Range from Range To Points Description
21 35 4
36 50 3
51 100 0

Description can be entered if required
In our example, if the applicant’s age is 27, then he/ she falls between the range “21-35” and hence will earn “4” points.
Click “Save” to save changes
Click “ cancel” to cancel the operation. Data entered so far will not be saved in this case.
Click “close” to return to the Corporate Rating screen
