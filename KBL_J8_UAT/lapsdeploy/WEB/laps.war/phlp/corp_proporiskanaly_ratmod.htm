Risk Analysis

Rating Model
This is the screen where the final consolidated rating on the corporate applicant is displayed. This is a screen with non-editable data. The parameters and rating from all the tabs in the “Rating analysis” is consolidated and displayed here as the final outcome/ judgment from the system on the applicant. Based on the result of the rating model, the decision maker can decide whether he wants to sanction the loan or not.
