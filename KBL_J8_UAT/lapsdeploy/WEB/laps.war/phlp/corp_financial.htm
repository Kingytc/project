Financial

The financial module of LAPS will be the most dynamic part of the software. The corporate office will define the balance sheet format based on the industry. The corporate office has also defined various formulas and ratios. All you have to do is enter the applicant’s financial data in the given format.

As mentioned above, a different financials format will be available for each industry (e.g. Software industry, Heavy engineering, leather industry etc). Consecutively the way the financial data is collected may differ according to the industry. This flexibility lies with the corporate office.

The “financials” tab will have multiple sub-tabs (e.g. Cost of sales, Income statement, Current assets, Current liabilities, Networth etc). The title/ nature of which may differ according to the industry. Saving data, canceling and closing the screen is similar to other tabs present in the corporate application form.
