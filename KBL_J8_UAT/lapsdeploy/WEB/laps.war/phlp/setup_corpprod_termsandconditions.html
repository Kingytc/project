Terms and Conditions
Necessary terms and conditions can be added for each facility under "Terms & conditions" in the SETUP module. The relevant list is displayed while a loan is processed. Along with this new terms can also be appended during the loan processing cycle depending on the applicant. For instance, the corporate applicant could be a new customer but possesses an excellent reputation or comes with an excellent recommendation from a very reputed customer. In such situations the Banker might want to give the loan but to reduce risk he may add an additional 'condition' of early repayment etc. The user when required can also delete any such term added. A list of such deleted terms & conditions can be seen at any point in time by clicking "Deleted terms" along with the reason for such an action

    Creating a set of new terms & conditions for a facility
    Click "Facility head" dropdown list
    Select the Facility head
    Click "Facility" dropdown list
    Select the relevant facility
    Click "New"
    Enter one term/ condition at a time in the empty text box provided in the screen.
    Click "Apply" every time you enter a new term/ condition
    Click "Cancel" to come out of the "New" mode. You will loose all entries made if you have not clicked "Add" before pressing Cancel
    Click "Close" to come back to the "Products" screen

    Editing an existing term/ condition
    Click "Facility head" dropdown list
    Select the facility head
    Click "Facility" dropdown list
    Select the relevant facility
    All the terms and conditions created for this facility will be displayed. There will be a radio button next to each of the term/ condition
    Click "Edit"
    Select the radio button next to the term/ condition you wish to Edit
    Now that item will be displayed in the empty text box above the listing
    Make necessary changes
    Click "Apply" to save changes
    Click "Delete" if you wish to delete the term/ condition right after you select the radio button
    Click "Close" to return to the Products screen
