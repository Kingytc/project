Industries Information and Rating (CoRc)

Other Information Tab
Apart from Financial ratios there are other Industry & economy related parameters that are used for evaluating the applicant’s performance. Those parameters, Key information about them and the units (monetary, Time, Percentage etc) is also noted.
To create a set of new parameters
Industry- Click the “?” next to the text box
Select the industry to which the applicant belongs
Date - Enter the date on which the records were created/ modified
Click “Save” to save the changes made
Click “cancel” to cancel the operation
Click the next tab or click “Close” to close the screen and go back to the “commercial rating screen


To edit an existing record
Select the Industry (same procedure as above)
Click “edit”
Make necessary changes
Click “Save” to save the changes made
Click “cancel” to cancel the operation
Click the next tab or click “Close” to close the screen and go back to the “commercial rating screen
