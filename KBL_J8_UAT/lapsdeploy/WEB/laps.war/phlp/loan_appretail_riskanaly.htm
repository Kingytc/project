Risk Analysis
Click the tab “Risk analysis” to see the credit rating carried out on the applicant based on the information you have filled into LAPS so far.
The Credit rating sheet displays
The parameters based on which the applicant has been evaluated
Score earned for each of the parameters
The particulars of the applicant for the parameters
Obtained total score
Cut off range within which the applicant’s total score falls
Grading scale for the range

Click the “Print preview” on the bottom of the page to see the print preview of the credit rating sheet. A print option is provided in the preview screen
