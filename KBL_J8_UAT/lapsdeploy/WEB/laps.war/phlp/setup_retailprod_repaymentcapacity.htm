Repayment Capacity
In this screen, LAPS determines the repayment capacity of the applicant based on business rules, namely,
Max limit of loan for an employed person - “X” times his/ her annual income
Max limit of loan for a self-employed person - “X” times his/ her annual income

    To enter new details
    Click “Edit”
    Max limit for loan (employed) - Depending on whether the applicant is employed with a concern or self-employed, the maximum limit recommended as loan amount can be only up to certain times the applicant’s annual income. This again varies from product to product. For instance, In a Housing loan for constructing a new house:
    •The maximum limit for loan if the applicant is employed could be 3 times his annual income
    •The maximum limit for loan if the applicant is self- employed could be 4 times his annual income

    Click “Apply” to save the data
    You can click “Cancel” to cancel changes carried out
    Click “Close” to return to the setup screen
