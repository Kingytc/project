Retail - risk management
Every loan application will be evaluated on various predefined risk parameters that vary depending on the loan type. The major categories are:
-Personal details
-Employment details
-Financial details
-Property details
-Other rules

Each category will evaluate the applicant on various parameters. Each such criterion will carry a score. The aggregate of these scores will be considered as the overall Rating for the applicant. Based on this score, LAPS will offer recommendation on “approval” or “rejection” of the loan. For instance a score between “59 - 82” could be rated as excellent while applying for a housing loan. You can define a Cutoff score for each retail loan product in the “Global options module. While the score ranges and the description for each range is defined in the “Cutoff score” module. Score ranges, description and cutoff score is soft and hence you can define it yourself.
