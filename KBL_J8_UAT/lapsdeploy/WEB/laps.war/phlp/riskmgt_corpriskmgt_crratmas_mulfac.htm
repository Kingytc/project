Credit Rating Raster

Multiplication Factor Tab

This screen is applicable only for Term loans. Longer the term of repayment more the risk is to the bank. To reduce this a numerical value is multiplied with the “Residual Maturity” of the term loan. To be precise different ranges of tenure are defined in this master, namely, the “Multiplication factor”. The range within which the Residual Factor of the Term loan in question is identified and the multiplication factor corresponding to this range is taken and multiplied with the score earned under the section “Borrower Financials”. You can create upto 5 different ranges and corresponding multiplication factors.

To create a new multiplication factor
Click “Edit”
Enter information
Tenure from - tenure to - Enter the tenure
Multiplication factor - Enter the multiplication factor
Click “Save” to save the changes made
Click “cancel” to cancel the operation
Click the next tab or click “Close” to close the screen and go back to the “commercial rating screen”

To edit existing details
Data is displayed in the screen in the form of a table
Click “Edit”
Make necessary changes
Click “Save” to save the changes made
Click “cancel” to cancel the operation
Click the next tab or click “Close” to close the screen and go back to the “commercial rating screen”
