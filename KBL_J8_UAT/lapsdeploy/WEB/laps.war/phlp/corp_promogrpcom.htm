Group Companies
You can enter details of associate/ subsidiary/ group companies of the applicant company
Adding group companies to the applicant organization
Click “New”
Enter the Name of the company/ firm
Enter the “Nature of activity”, in other words the business carried out in the group company
Enter details of the “Banking arrangement” (you can enter the name of the group company’s banker) for the group company.
Click “Apply” to save the data entered
Click “cancel” if you want to cancel the operation. Remember that you will lose any data entered.
Click “close” to go back to the search screen
Editing an existing record
All records created are displayed below the control buttons
There is a radio button adjacent to each record. Click the radio button next to the record you wish to edit
Now click “Edit”
The record appears in the space where you entered data originally
Make necessary changes
Click “Apply” to save the data entered
Click “cancel” if you want to cancel the operation. Remember that you will lose any changes made
Click “close” to go back to the search screen
