Insurance policies
Click "Insurance Policies" sub-tab
Details of insurance policies held by the applicant or co applicant can be entered using ‘Insurance policies’ sub tab.

Enter data in the columns provided
Policy Number: Enter the Insurance policy number here. Normally the policy number is that number which the Insurance provider requests the user to quote for all communications
Maturity Date: Enter the date on which the applicant becomes eligible to get the promised money from the Insurance agent
Last Premium Paid up to: Enter the date up to, which the premium has been paid to the insurance company
Surrender/ paidup Value: Enter the surrender value of the insurance policy. This is the Amount the Applicant can get if he opts to surrender his insurance as on date to the Insurance company
Insurance company Name: Enter the name of the company from where the Insurance policy has been taken
Sum Assured: Enter the amount assured to the applicant excluding the bonus, which he will acquire in the course of time
Applicant Type: Click the drop down box
The following options are displayed: Applicant, Co-applicant, and Guarantor. If the co-applicant or guarantor is not present for this application then they do not figure on the list
Select the person to whom the Insurance policy belongs
Collateral: Click on the radio button if, you wish to offer the mentioned Insurance policy as collateral
A property can be offered as collateral irrespective of whether it belongs to the applicant/ co-applicant or the guarantor. A maximum of 8 Insurance policies can be entered in this screen.
Once all required Investment details are entered, Click Apply to save the details entered.
