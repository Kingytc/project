Liability Tab

Click "Liability" sub-tab
Institution - Enter the name of the organization/ bank/ financial institution from where the applicant has acquired the mentioned liability. This is an alphanumeric field
Liability type - Click the drop down box
The following options are displayed; Credit card, Line of credit, overdraft, Personal loan, others
Select the suitable liability type
Account no - Enter the account number allotted by the concerned institution for the above-mentioned liability. This is a numeric field
Loan amount - Enter the original liable amount in monetary terms (in Rs), In the case of credit card liability, this can be the same as the “minimum monthly payment”. This is a numeric field
Balance - Enter the amount that is due or in other words that is yet to be paid to the institution. This is a numeric field
Monthly/ Minimum Payment -Enter the monthly payment due for the liability. In the case of “Credit card” mention minimum payment. This is a numeric field
Applicant type - Click the drop down box
The following options are displayed: applicant/ co-applicant or guarantor. If the co-applicant or guarantor is not present for this application then they do not figure on the list

Select the party who is liable
