Used by

    Click “Edit” to enter data
    This option enables the user to define the reach of the retail loan product within the bank. In other words you can pinpoint and choose various locations where the loan product will be offered to the public.

    Select Organization level: There is a radio button given near each organization level, namely, Corporate, zone, region and branches. Click on the button to select the desired organization level, where this loan product should be made available.

    Select the institution: Once you select the organization level, the institutions under the organizational level get displayed in this field in a list box. Double click on the desired institution that can use. Alternatively select, “Corporate” so that the loan product is made available to the entire bank.

    Selected institution: The institution selected through the above field gets listed automatically in this list box.
    Click “Apply” to save changes
    You can click “Cancel” to cancel changes carried out
    Click “Close” to return to the setup screen
