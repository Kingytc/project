Appraisal
The appraisal note is generated automatically in a printable format using information that has been already filled in by the applicant in the application form. The appraisal note has the following fields:
Applicant, Co Applicant, Guarantor, Purpose of Loan, Category, Sub category, Facility limit - All these fields are automatically populated into the printable “appraisal note” form the details already filled in the application form
Rate of Interest - The interest that is proposed to be charged to the applicant
Purchase cost - This field displays the cost of the proposed asset
Repayment schedule -An amortization schedule that has been worked out automatically by LAPS forms the basis for this and also the type of repayment schedule the has chosen
Period - The repayment period in which the applicant has agreed to repay the loan amount to BoB
Facility - User to fill in whether the proposed facility will be a d demand loan or term loan or overdraft as the case maybe
EMI - Equated monthly Installments calculated by LAPS based on the loan amount, repayment term and interest rate
Commencing from - The date from which the repayment schedule commences, this field requires to be filled in by the user
Risk Rating - The result of the credit rating that has been carried out on the applicant is populated here automatically in the form of the “Score” and “Grade” he/ she has earned
Securities Documents - This field displays the list of documents that have been submitted from both the applicant’s side and the bank’s side
Terms & conditions - This field displays the list of terms & conditions that have been stipulated for the loan product
Appraisal note - Enter your appraisal note in this alphanumeric field
Comments of sanctioning authority - The approving authority can record his/ her comments on the loan. This is a user enterable alphanumeric field
