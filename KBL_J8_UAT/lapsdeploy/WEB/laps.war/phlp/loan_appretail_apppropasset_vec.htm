Vehicle
Click “Vehicle” sub-tab
Category - Click the drop down box
Choose between the two options, namely, New vehicle, Used vehicle
Registration number - Enter the registration number of the vehicle
Make & Model - Enter the make and model of the vehicle offered as collateral. For example, Make - 911 and the model - Porsche
Name of dealer/ seller - Enter the name of the automobile dealer from whom the vehicle was purchased in this alphanumeric field. For instance Shakthi Automobiles
S. No - Enter the Engine/ chassis number of the vehicle
Age of vehicle - Enter the age of the vehicle in this numeric field
Type of Vehicle - Click the drop down box
Choose between the options, namely, > 4 lakhs, between 4 and 6 lakhs and > 6 lakhs,
This determines the market segment to which the vehicle belongs.
Approximate serviceable life of the vehicle - The number of years the make & model vehicle can be serviced before it becomes obsolete
Valuation as per garage certificate - Value of the vehicle as on date as certified by an authorized service agent
Last life tax paid - Enter the date on which life tax was last paid for the vehicle. Enter the date in the DD/MM/YYYY format, i.e., Date/ Month/ Year format
Last tax paid - Enter the date in which tax was last paid for the vehicle. Enter the date in the DD/MM/YYYY format, i.e., Date/ Month/ Year format
