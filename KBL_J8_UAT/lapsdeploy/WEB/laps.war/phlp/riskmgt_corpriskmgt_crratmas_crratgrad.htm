Credit Rating Raster

Credit Rating Grade
Recommendation o the credit worthiness of a potential corporate borrower is arrived at by the system by allotting scores for various parameters by which the borrower was evaluated. The Total of the scores earned by the potential borrower is compared against the “Credit Rating grade” created by the bank in this screen

To create a new set of “credit rating grades”
Click “New”
Nature of grade - Enter a nature of grade for every range of score in this field.
Score from - To - Enter the range within which the scores will fall and be evaluated in these numeric fields
Definition of Grade - Enter a description for every score range in this alphanumeric field. For instance,
Score between 35 - 49, Nature = “A”, Def. of Grade = Doubtful or very high prob. of default
Score between 85 - 100, Nature = “AAA”, Def. of Grade = Highest safety

Click “Save” to save the changes made
Click “cancel” to cancel the operation
Click “Close” to close the screen and go back to the “commercial rating screen”

To edit an existing score range
Click the radio button adjacent to the score range
The score range will appear in the table where data was entered
Click “Edit”

Make necessary changes
Click “Save” to save the changes made
Click “cancel” to cancel the operation
Click “Close” to close the screen and go back to the “commercial rating screen”
