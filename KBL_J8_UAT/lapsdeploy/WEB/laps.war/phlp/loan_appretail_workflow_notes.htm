Notes
The credit officer can record his/ her comments on the loan application. Comments previously entered are also displayed.
Click “Edit”
Enter comments in the “Present comments” text box. Click “Enter” to enter data in the next line
Click “Apply”
The comments you had entered gets populated automatically in the text box, “Previous comments” immediately along with the LAPS user ID and current system date
Thus the credit officer can enter comments from time to time during the loan processing cycle.
