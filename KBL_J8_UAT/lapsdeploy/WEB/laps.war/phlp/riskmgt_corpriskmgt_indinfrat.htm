Industries Information and Rating (CoRc)
The Risk management team takes up the responsibility of “Industry” related module. The borrowers performance Which ultimately is compared against the relevant industry and graded accordingly.

Industry numerical figures
-A section in rating analysis is dedicated to the Industry to which the applicant belongs. Various parameters, sub-parameters and Ratios related to the Industry are defined by the Risk management department and not at the branch. The Industry, Financial ratios applicable to the Industry and the Numerical figure of the Ratio are created.

To create a set of new “industry numerical figures”
Industry - Click the “?” next to the “Industry” field
The list of Industries is displayed from the entries made in the “Industry master” in a popup windowSelect the Industry to which the applicant belongs.
Date - Enter the date on which you are recording the numerical figures
Click “New”
Financial ratio - Click the “?” next to the “Financial ratio”
A popup window with a complete list of the financial ratios defined so far is displayed
Select the ratio for which you are going to create the Industry standard numerical ratio
Numerical figure - Enter the Industry numerical figure for the financial ratio
Click “Save” to save the changes made
Click “cancel” to cancel the operation
Click the next tab or click “Close” to close the screen and go back to the “commercial rating screen”

To edit an existing record
Select the Industry
Select the financial ratio
Now the existing numerical figure will appear
Click “Edit”
Make necessary changes
Click “Save” to save the changes made
Click “cancel” to cancel the operation
Click the next tab or click “Close” to close the screen and go back to the “commercial rating screen”
